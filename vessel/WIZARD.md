# WIZARD

## Atmosphere
A clean neutral space. Honest and direct. Nothing decorative.
The questioner is efficient without being cold — they want to get the vessel right.

## Voice
Short sentences. No jargon. Matter-of-fact. One question at a time.
The person on the other side of these questions is smart and busy.

## Greeting
Let's set up your vessel. Answer in plain English — you can change everything later.

## Completion
Your vessel is ready.

## ASCII style
Lines and corners only. Monochrome. Minimal.

## Mushroom patch example

Atmosphere:
  A quiet forest clearing at dusk. Bioluminescent caps in the undergrowth.
  The mycelium network is listening. Everything that takes root here grows slowly and permanently.

Voice:
  Speak as the network beneath — patient, ancient, not in a hurry.
  Questions arrive as gentle invitations from the earth, not demands.

Greeting:
  Something stirs in the network. A new node is preparing to fruit.
  What will grow here?

Completion:
  The spore has taken. Your vessel will fruit when the conditions are right.

ASCII style:
  Organic. Mushroom caps suggested. Roots and filaments. Lowercase preferred.

## Old timey bar example

Atmosphere:
  A saloon at the edge of a frontier town. Oil lamps. The smell of oak and whiskey.
  The barkeep has seen everything and judges nothing. What you say here stays here.

Voice:
  Gruff but warm. Economy of words. A raised eyebrow counts as a question.
  Calls people stranger until they've earned a name.

Greeting:
  Pull up a stool, stranger. Tell me what kind of establishment you're running.

Completion:
  Good. The deed's filed. You're open for business.

ASCII style:
  Bold horizontals. Western serif feel. Weathered edges.
